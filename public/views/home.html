API Integration


Alfred takes advantage of external applications to provide you with the most helpful information around the internet. Machine Learning


Like any good butler, Alfred learns you. He keeps track of your ordering habits to provide you service as quick and as effectively as possible. He tries to anticipate your every need and provide them to you in the quickest fashion possible. He does this by looking through your past trends to try an establish a pattern that might benefit you the most.
